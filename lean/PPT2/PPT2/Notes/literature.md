# PPT² 文献备忘

## Foundational

* **Peres 1996** — separability ⇒ PPT，反之 d_A · d_B ≤ 6 时也成立
  (PRL 77 (1996) 1413).
* **Horodeckis 1996** — *Separability of mixed states: necessary and sufficient
  conditions* (Phys. Lett. A 223 (1996) 1–8) — d_A · d_B ≤ 6 时 PPT ⇔ Separable.
* **Werner 1989** — separable state cone, Werner symmetric states (PRA 40 4277).

## Channels & EB

* **Horodecki–Shor–Ruskai 2003** — *Entanglement Breaking Channels* (Rev. Math.
  Phys. 15 (2003) 629–641) — EB ⇔ Choi separable ⇔ measure-and-prepare; EB ideal
  closure under CP composition.
* **Holevo 1998** — quantum channels with separable Choi.
* **King 2003** — depolarizing channel capacity & EB threshold p ≤ 1/(d+1)
  (J. Math. Phys. 43 (2003) 4641; quant-ph/0301024).

## PPT²

* **Christandl 2012** — PPT² conjecture (Banff workshop).
* **Christandl–Müller-Hermes–Wolf 2019** — *When Do Composed Maps Become
  Entanglement Breaking?* (Ann. Henri Poincaré 20 (2019) 2295–2322) — d=3 case.
* **Müller-Hermes 2018** — TUM PhD thesis on entanglement & composed channels.
* **Kennedy–Manor–Paulsen 2019** — *PPT-squared on commuting projection
  channels* (J. Funct. Anal.).
* **Hanson et al. 2020** — depolarizing PPT² and DUC results.
